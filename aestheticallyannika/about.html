About the Artist

Annika Nicole Wade is a third-generation artist from Central Arkansas who has had a passion for creating things since she started learning how to talk.

Her favorite mediums are oil & gouache paints, ceramics, and digital art, but she also enjoys ink & charcoal illustrations and woodworking, and has a curiosity for anything art-related.

Her newest series of work focuses on mental health and "breaking the cycle" of generational trauma.

She is cat-mom to Ivy, Delilah, and Prisma and enjoys spending her free time at Goodwill, out in nature, or meditating.
